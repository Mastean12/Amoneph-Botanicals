Who We Are

Amoneph Botanicals is a Kenyan natural wellness brand committed to providing pure, nutrient-rich, and affordable products sourced directly from trusted local farmers. We specialize in delivering high-quality honey, nutrient-dense nut butters, herbal wellness powders, and premium cold-pressed oils.

Our mission is to elevate everyday health through pure, unprocessed, and naturally effective products that support modern lifestyles while honoring traditional Kenyan wellness practices.

Our Founder's Story

Growing up in rural Kenya, I witnessed firsthand the healing power of nature. My grandmother would use local herbs, honey, and oils to treat common ailments. This traditional wisdom, combined with modern wellness practices, inspired me to create Amoneph Botanicals.

Our commitment is simple: to provide pure, accessible natural products that honor our Kenyan heritage while meeting modern wellness needs. Every product tells a story of community, sustainability, and natural healing.

- The Amoneph Team
